Max M. Jackson, M.Ed. is an experienced professional from higher education, considering working for an educational technology firm or a university, as a programmer, web developer, or applications systems analyst. He thoroughly enjoys collaborating on ideas, and building, testing, and deploying tools and applications with colleagues. Max is skilled at speaking the language of the ‘tech-world’ and the ‘world of education.’ Max would be an asset to an organization seeking a liaison to bridge the two areas via full-stack web development. Max completed his web development certificate in 2019 at the University of Arizona in Tucson.



Read My Bio

Hi everybody! My name is Max and I live in Tucson, AZ. I am an Arizona native and grew up in the eastern part of the state. In my free time I like to watch baseball and follow the Boston Red Sox, visit art museums, as well as hike, swim, and spend time with my nephews and nieces.

I began studying full stack web development in January, 2019 and I love every minute of it. No longer do I dread reading graduate-level theoretical text. Rather, I am eager to find free time to work on applications to learn web development. They say it's not work if you love what you do!

Professionally, I have been working for the University of Arizona's SALT Center for twelve and a half years. Currently, I am the Admissions Coordiantor, and we provide academic support to UA students who learn differntly.

I am an alumnus of the University of Arizona with a B.A. in Art History. I graduated from Northern Arizona University in 2016 with my Master's Degree in Educational Leadership, CCHE (Community College and Higher Education).

Please check out the rest of my web development portfolio and contact me if you'd like to network!
