Project 1 - Editorial Tone

An API-powered web application that pulls in New York Times articles and an analyzes the article's 'emotional tone'. Users are also given a link to the article to read.

Project 2 - TRAINr

TRAINr is your personal workout tracking app. It helps track your workout and allows for easy analysis of your progress, to keep you motivated and stick to your routine!

Eat Da Burger

Type a burger name, click 'Place your order'. It moves to 'My Plate'. Click 'Devour it' to "eat da burger". The "devoured" burger will then moves to 'My compliments to the chef'.



Friend Finder

Click a 'survey' button. Give your name & a link to your image. Answer 10 questions. The app compares your answers with potential friends, to display your "best match".

New York Times Search Engine

Interfacing with the New York Times API; in-class small-group project to practice using APIs. Created by Michael Garcia, Ryan Grady, and Max Jackson

Scary Movie Trivia

Scary move-themed and timed questions. A correct or wrong answer, or timeout, retrieves the next question. The final screen shows a percentage 'grade'.



Gif-Tastic!

Click a button to grab 10 static, non-animated gifs from the GIPHY API. When the user clicks a still GIPHY image, the gif animates. If the user clicks the gif, it stops.

Crystal Collector

Four crystals are displayed with a random number, when a crystal is clicked the amount is added to the user's total. The user wins if the total score matches the random number.

The Psychic Game

The app randomly picks a letter, & the user guesses which letter the app chose. This app features dynamically updated HTML & CSS powered by the JavaScript code.



Train-Scheduler

A Firebase train schedule to host arrival and departure data. It manipulates info with Moment.js, & provides current data about a train's arrival time & minutes until arrival.

Rock, Paper, Scissors Game

The user will make a choice & then the compter will make a choice. The app will collect the choices, compare the choices & display wins, losses, ties.
